Overview

Take your projects anywhere with this 13" Microsoft Surface laptop, a smart, portable machine that keeps up. Featuring super-long battery life, you can keep going all day with no interruptions. This Copilot+ PC with turbocharged NPU delivers powerful performance and AI accelerated power. Plus, enjoy an immersive viewing experience with the stunning PixelSense Flow display.

  • Surface Laptop
  • USB-C Charging Cable
